The Artists

A small, hand-picked roster of Lisbon-based makers — each represented exclusively, each invited for the singularity of their voice.

Ceramics

Ana Martins

Working from a sunlit atelier in Graça, Ana reinterprets the rhythm of Portuguese azulejos through hand-built porcelain. Her pieces are held in private collections from Lisbon to Tokyo.

Painting

Ricardo Fonte

Ricardo captures the specific quality of Lisbon light — that golden hour refraction off the Tejo — in large-format oil on linen. A graduate of the Escola Superior de Belas-Artes, his work has been exhibited across Europe.

Photography & Drawing

Silvia Rocha

Silvia moves fluidly between documentary photography and charcoal drawing, finding the architecture of shadow in Alfama's narrow lanes. Her work is both intimate and monumental.
