Artist Spotlight

"Lisbon is a permanent dialogue between the river and the tile, between fado and the future."

Working from a sunlit atelier in Graça, Ana Martins reinterprets the rhythm of Portuguese azulejos through hand-built porcelain. Her pieces are held in private collections from Lisbon to Tokyo.
