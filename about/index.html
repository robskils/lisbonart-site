About us

Art rooted in
one city.

LisbonArt.pt was founded on a simple premise: that Lisbon has produced a generation of artists whose work deserves a proper international audience.

We represent a deliberately small roster — artists whose work is not merely inspired by Lisbon, but is inseparable from it. The light off the Tejo, the geometry of azulejos, the drama of Alfama's shadows. Work you couldn't mistake for anywhere else.

Everything we sell is an original or a numbered limited edition. Every piece ships with a certificate of authenticity and full provenance record. We stand behind every work we sell.

🏛️
"We don't sell souvenirs. We sell the real thing — work that took years to learn, months to make, and will last a lifetime on your wall."

How we work

What we believe

Exclusivity earns trust

We represent each artist exclusively. That means we're fully invested in their career and their reputation — and that investment protects yours.

Small is intentional

A small roster means we know every work intimately. We can answer any question about a piece, its context, its creation.

Online and in-person

We believe art should be seen in the flesh before it's bought. Our pop-ups exist to let you do that — in the city that produced the work.

Provenance forever

Every certificate we issue is permanently registered. In 30 years, when you want to resell, the record is there.

Get in touch

We're here

Questions about a specific work, enquiries about representation, press requests, or just want to say hello — we read and reply to every email.
